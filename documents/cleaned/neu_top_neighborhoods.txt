Northeastern University is a private research university with its main campus in Boston, Massachusetts, United States. With more than 38,000 students, Northeastern is the largest university in Massachusetts by enrollment!  When searching for off‑campus housing near Northeastern University in Boston, students have several vibrant neighborhood options—each with unique perks.Check out the top neighborhoods for incoming Northeastern Students living in Boston.

1. Symphony / Fenway–Kenmore

Location & commute: Situated along Huntington Avenue, just a short walk or MBTA ride to campus. Easily serviced by the Green Line, buses, and nearby Kenmore Square.

Vibe & amenities: A mix of student housing and professionals, with access to Fenway Park, shops, and restaurants.

Rent & housing: Offers a wide range—from studios to multi-bedroom units—though Fenway–Kenmore tends toward mid-to-high price ranges

2. Back Bay

Walkability: A stylish, upscale area with tree‑lined streets, historic brownstones, boutiques, and cafés—all just a mile or so from campus.

Recommended for: Students seeking a more cosmopolitan living experience—but note rents trend on the higher side.

3. South End

Character: Renowned for its Victorian brownstones, leafy parks, art galleries, and a dynamic dining scene. It’s a landmark district loved for its charm.

Commute: A pleasant ~15-minute walk or brief MBTA ride to Northeastern.

Community feel: A vibrant mix of young professionals and students, perfect for those after a cultured yet friendly area

4. Mission Hill

Affordability: Known to be more budget-friendly than Fenway or Back Bay.

Transit: Served well by the Green Line (“E” branch) and Orange Line via Roxbury Crossing, making campus commutes manageable.

Neighborhood perks: Historic architecture, brick triple-deckers, proximity to Longwood Medical Area, and a diverse local culture with plenty of eateries and conveniences

5. Kenmore

Overlap with Fenway: Often considered part of the broader Fenway–Kenmore area—this neighborhood is ideal for commuters.

Student hub: Home to students from Northeastern and Boston University, offering apartment options close to Kenmore Square and the T.
